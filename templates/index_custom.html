This project focuses on analyzing online reviews to detect fake or spam content using various machine learning techniques. By examining text patterns, reviewer behavior, and other metrics, we aim to provide an efficient solution to detect fake reviews that could impact the reputation of products or services.

Why Fake Review Analysis?

Fake reviews are a growing concern in the digital world. Consumers often rely on reviews to make informed purchasing decisions, but false reviews can easily deceive them. This project aims to provide a tool that helps businesses and users identify and filter out fake reviews, ensuring a more trustworthy online shopping experience.
